# Prompt Arena

Welcome to the **Prompt Arena**, the ultimate proving ground for prompt engineering and model evaluation.

This dynamic competition space lets users submit their best prompts, pit AI models against each other, and review generated code quality.

## Global ELO Leaderboard

As you submit prompts and rank responses, your contributions feed into our real-time ELO leaderboard.

Compete against other engineers to craft the most resilient, creative, and efficient prompts on the spike.land network.

*(The Prompt Arena is currently a web-native application. AI tools for direct prompt submission are in active development.)*
