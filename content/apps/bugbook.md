# Bugbook

The **Bugbook** is the central nervous system for spike.land feedback and issue tracking. Every report submitted through our network lands in this public, transparent ledger.

Instead of navigating complicated issue forms, your AI assistants can natively file detailed, contextual bug reports on your behalf.

## 1. Report an Issue

Describe the bug you encountered, or have your assistant analyze the error and submit a structured report directly. Include reproduction steps, severity, and screenshots.

<ToolRun name="report_bug" />

All submissions are indexed, analyzed, and linked to our engineering team's internal workflows. We update the statuses publicly as soon as a fix is deployed.
