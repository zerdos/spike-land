## The Premise

Zoltán brought a high-frequency challenge to the Arena: **The Falsetto Peak**.

"Smalltown Boy" (Bronski Beat, 1984) is a masterclass in using frequency to convey isolation and liberation. The goal was to build a tool that visualizes Jimmy Somerville's falsetto as a physical trajectory. If the chorus "Run away" is a flight, the interface needs to show the lift.

**Arena rules:** High-resolution pitch tracking. Color-coded syllable weights. Integration of "Hi-NRG" visual kinetics.

**Personas:** Zoltán (the historian), Radix (the frequency analyst), Daft Punk (the high-energy technicians), Einstein (the researcher of Doppler shifts).

---

## Round 1 — Zoltán

"Nézd, the power of this song is in the escape. The suitcase, the platform, the rain. I want the colors to reflect that '80s neon sadness. Pink for the heart, gold for the sunrise. The syllables shouldn't just light up; they should strike like a pulse."

## Round 2 — Radix

"We mapped the pitch contour using Jimmy's vocal track. The verses sit at a standard range ($0.4 - 0.65$ normalized), but the chorus peaks hit $0.95$. We need a **Pitch Bar** that visualizes these peaks as a 'runway'. It gives the singer a visual target for those high notes."

## Round 3 — Daft Punk

"The intro is pure Hi-NRG synth. To match that energy, we added an **Ambient Glow** engine. Two orbs that breathe inversely to the audio amplitude. When the beat drops in at 0:48, the orbs' opacity doubles. It’s a multi-sensory sync."

## Round 4 — Einstein

"Emotional themes often resonate at specific frequencies. By adding **Theta** and **Delta** binaural beats, we allow the user to experience the 'Run away' theme not as a panic, but as a deliberate state shift. We are analyzing the physics of leaving home to find yourself."

## The Escape Formula

The Arena modeled the relationship between the vocal pitch $P$ and the emotional resonance $R$:

$$R(t) = \int_{Chorus} \left( \frac{P(t) - P_{base}}{P_{peak} - P_{base}} \right)^2 dt$$

The 'Run away' peak is the moment $R(t)$ reaches its maximum, triggering the full bloom of the ambient glow.
