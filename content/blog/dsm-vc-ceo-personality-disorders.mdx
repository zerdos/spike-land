## 1. Founder's Delusion (*Delusionus Foundrus*)

Patient believes they are the next Steve Jobs. Evidence: owns the same turtleneck. Contradicting evidence: Juicero. Symptoms escalate after reading one (1) Paul Graham essay. **Prognosis:** terminal, treated only by a failed Series B.

## 2. Chronic Visionitis

Cannot describe the company without the word "disrupting." Has disrupted dog food, toothbrushes, and — most recently — the concept of Tuesdays. When asked what the product *does*, eyes glaze and mouth emits the phrase *"we're building the rails for the future of…"* before short-circuiting.

## 3. LinkedIn Narcissistic Personality Disorder (LNPD)

Every post begins with **"Humbled and honored to announce…"**. Has been humbled 847 times this quarter. Each humbling involves a photo with better lighting than their wedding. Acute flare-ups triggered by TechCrunch mentions.

## 4. Synergy Tourette's

Involuntary emission of the words *synergy*, *leverage*, *north star*, and *delta* mid-sentence. In severe cases: **"Let's circle back offline to leverage our learnings on the delta of the north star."** Linguists have classified this as a separate dialect: **MBAese.**

## 5. Open-Door / Shut-Mouth Syndrome

"My door is always open." Door is open. CEO is not inside. When located, interrupts your sentence at word three and tells you what you were about to say. You were not about to say that.

## 6. Pivot Compulsion Disorder

Company was a dating app. Then blockchain. Then SaaS for dentists. Then AI for dentists. Then AI. Cap table looks like a Jackson Pollock. Each pivot is announced as *"returning to our roots."* There are no roots. There is only pivot.

## 7. 4 AM Thinkfluencer Dysmorphia

Tweets at 04:12: *"While you were sleeping, I was building."* They were not building. They were doomscrolling and eating cold pad thai. The grind is performative. The pad thai is real.

## 8. All-Hands Histrionics

Announces layoffs of 30% of the company with a **single tear**. Ends meeting with *"let's go crush it, team."* The team has been crushed. Six weeks later: yacht photo. Caption: *"Grateful."*

## 9. Acquisition Hoarding Disorder

Has acquired 14 startups in 18 months. Uses 0 of them. The engineers have been "integrated into a strategic initiative," which is the corporate equivalent of Narnia. The products now redirect to a 404 with a sad logo.

## 10. Board Deck Munchausen

Invents crises so they can heroically solve them at the next board meeting. *"We faced an unprecedented headwind in Q3."* They were the headwind. They are also, somehow, the tailwind. Truly multidimensional weather.

## 11. Vesting Cliff Dependency

Loves the company intensely until month 47. Discovers "a new calling" exactly at month 48 and one day. New calling is always a fund. The fund's thesis is *"backing exceptional founders."* Mirror not included.

## 12. Retweet-Induced Grandiosity

Marc Andreessen liked their tweet once in 2021. They have not recovered. Bio now reads: *"As featured by a16z."* Feature was a heart emoji.

---

## Differential Diagnosis

If the patient exhibits **any three** of the above simultaneously, they are not sick — **they are a keynote speaker.** Please direct them to the nearest conference in Lisbon.

## Treatment

There is no cure. There is only the next round.
