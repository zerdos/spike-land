## 🛠️ The "How-To" Deep Dive

Why is this so fast? In most fullstack development, you spend 80% of your time writing glue code. Connecting the database to the API, the API to the frontend, the frontend to the state management.

With Spike Land MCPs, the **logic is already glue-less**. Every tool listed above is a stateless, discoverable function. When you describe an app to our AI builder, it’s not just generating UI; it’s generating the **intent** to call existing, hardened protocols.

For example, when building **Grandmaster Arena**, the AI doesn't need to know how ELO is calculated. It just knows it needs to call the `chess-player` MCP tool to get the rating. 

This is the future of development. Not just more code, but more **meaningful** code.
