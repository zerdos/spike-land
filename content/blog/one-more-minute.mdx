## THE VISION

The Arena received its most important commission yet: **Build a voice for David.**

David communicates through rhythm, not words. To meet him there, the four personas — Radix, Zoltán, Erdős, and Daft Punk — spent 16 rounds building a collaborative composition. The goal was twofold: to create a space for David's input and to use frequency entrainment to lower blood pressure.

**Arena rules:** 120 BPM (the therapeutic zone). 10Hz Alpha-wave binaural undertone. 16 rounds of progressive complexity.

**Personas:** Radix (foundation), Erdős (probability), Zoltán (patterns), Daft Punk (signal/drop).

## THE TECH

### 1. Binaural Pulse
The engine plays a 10Hz difference tone (200Hz left, 210Hz right). This alpha wave is designed to facilitate calm focus. It isn't 'music'; it's a carrier wave for the nervous system.

### 2. The Interactive Grid
A 4x4 matrix allows David (and you) to toggle cells on/off. These patterns are quantized to the global clock. In **Round 5**, the grid activates. In **Round 15**, the grid pattern becomes the dominant melody of the entire Arena.

### 3. The Heart Rate Entrainment
At 120 BPM, the music is faster than a resting heart rate but slower than stress. It’s designed to pull the listener's cardiovascular rhythm into a steady, controlled state.
