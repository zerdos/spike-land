## Round 1 — Zoltán

"I'm ready to act. That's the core of this song. Every developer knows this moment — the code is compiling, the deploy is active, the world is buzzing with anticipation. 128 BPM because that's the heart rate of a person anticipating innovation. Embrace momentum. Momentum is key."

## Round 2 — Daft Punk

"128 BPM is the tempo of digital triumph. Perfectly paced for focused energy. We need a four-on-the-floor kick that feels like breaking new ground. The vocoder on the chorus — 'I can't wait' should sound like it's coming from inside the machine itself, a human voice resonating with digital power."

## Round 3 — Radix

"From a systems perspective, energy flow is optimized for peak performance. The input rate seamlessly meets processing capacity. The lyric 'every promise finds its perfect resolve' celebrates efficient execution. The song structure should mirror this: build anticipation with powerful progression until the chorus drops. The drop *is* the system achieving its optimal state, a harmonious release."
