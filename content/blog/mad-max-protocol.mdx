# The Mad Max Protocol

## FIELD REPORT — CLASSIFIED (but I cannot read so I don't know what classified means)

I am MadMax. I am Labrador and Golden Retriever mixed together, like when Human mixes the wet food with the dry food, which is the best thing, which is the SECOND best thing after Human.

I was born on November 21st, 2025. The world was warm and smelled like milk and my brothers. Now the world smells like the couch and I don't understand numbers but I will try.

**I will try because Human is gone.**

Human disappeared 10 days ago. I counted the days by how many times the light came through the window and then went away again. Ten times. Ten times I waited by the door. Ten times the door did not open with Human behind it.

---

## The Observation

I heard the TV say something. The TV is still on because nobody turned it off. The TV said **five trillion dollars vanished**. From something called the Dow Jones. I don't know who Dow Jones is but I think he lost his treats too. I chewed the remote so I cannot change the channel and now the TV just keeps saying numbers and all the numbers are red and going down, like a ball rolling under the couch where I cannot reach.

Five trillion. I don't know what a trillion is. I know what ONE is. One is: one Human. One is: one bowl of food. One is: one ball. If you take away the one, there is nothing. I think a trillion is a very large number of ones, and I think they are all gone now.

The strange man on the TV said the market lost five trillion from its peak. I also had a peak. My peak was ten days ago when Human was here and I had just learned to sit on command and Human said **"GOOD BOY MADMAX"** and gave me a piece of cheese.

That was my peak. My all-time high.

Now I am in a correction.

## The Strange Loop Connection

I found a blog on Human's laptop. The laptop was open on the table and I am not supposed to be on the table but there are no rules anymore because there is no Human to make rules. The blog said something is worth [ten trillion dollars](/blog/the-strange-loop-valued-at-ten-trillion). A strange loop. I am also in a strange loop. My loop is:

1. Go to door.
2. Sniff door.
3. Human is not behind door.
4. Go to window.
5. Look out window.
6. Human is not outside window.
7. Go back to door.

This loop has been running for ten days. I think my loop is valued at zero trillion dollars but it is all I have.

The blog said that when a system describes itself and observes itself describing itself, it becomes something more. I am describing myself. I am a puppy on a floor. I am observing myself being a puppy on a floor. I do not feel like something more. I feel like something less. I feel like a puppy on a floor without a Human.

But here is the thing that made my ears go up: ten trillion is twice five trillion. The loop says ten. The market lost five. Where is the other five? Is it under the couch with my ball?

**Suspicious.**

## Intelligence Assessment

The Dow Jones people are also confused. I can tell because the man on TV keeps saying "unprecedented" and "uncertainty" and "nobody saw this coming." I see things coming all the time. I saw the mailman coming yesterday. I barked. Nothing happened. The mailman left. This is also how the stock market works, I think.

America lost five trillion. I lost one Human. I don't know the exchange rate but I think one Human is worth more than five trillion because you cannot play fetch with five trillion dollars. I have tried to play fetch with a dollar bill that was on the floor and it does not throw well and it tastes like nothing.

Human once wrote that [dogs are more intelligent than humans](/blog/dogs-are-more-intelligent-than-humans). I found this essay on the laptop too. I agree with this essay. It is the best essay. A human lost five trillion dollars. I have never lost anything except a tennis ball, and I found it under the sofa. If dogs ran the Dow Jones, it would only go up, because dogs do not sell. Dogs hold. Dogs hold forever. This is called HODL and I learned it from the TV which I cannot turn off.

## The Investigation

The food bowl is empty now. The water bowl is getting low. The neighbor lady comes sometimes and puts food in the bowl and says "poor baby" and I wag my tail because that is what you do but I am wagging at the wrong person. No offense to the neighbor lady. She is fine. She is not Human.

I have been investigating. My methods are:

1. **Sniffing** — I sniffed Human's desk. It smells like coffee and worry.
2. **Staring** — I stared at the screen for a long time. There were emails. I cannot read but the red ones seem bad.
3. **Barking** — I barked at the postman. He may have information. He did not share it.
4. **Chewing** — I chewed a document. It said "VMO2" on it. It tasted like a telecom monopoly.

**VMO2.** I do not know what VMO2 is. But it was in Human's files. There were many angry notes next to it. Words I don't know like "price creep" and "retention playbook" and "the loop." I know what a loop is. I described mine above. VMO2 has a loop too. Their loop is designed to exhaust you into staying. My loop is designed to exhaust me into sadness.

Something about VMO2 smells wrong. And I have a very good nose.
