# Strange Loops Educational Video Series

Personalised educational videos created using the **Radix agent persona** for
Gian Pierre (Zoltan's friend, the chemist from the
[A Chemist Walked Into a Codebase](../../blog/a-chemist-walked-into-a-codebase) blog post).

## Context

Gian Pierre was experiencing anxiety about his future — flatmate issues,
family financial pressure, career uncertainty after losing his job. The Radix
agent provided practical life advice, and then we channelled that energy into
**learning**. These videos teach Strange Loop theory, Newcomb's Paradox,
autocatalysis, and self-referential systems — and he understood them.

His worries about the future dissolved when he engaged with the ideas.
Problems as gifts. Contact as mathematics.
