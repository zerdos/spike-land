================================================================================
PRD: "Elvis, We Love You" — A NotebookLM Video
From Zoltan, Radix Mental Health Wing, March 2026
================================================================================

PURPOSE
-------
A short, warm video for Elvis — a French-speaking guy in the Radix mental
health wing with Zoltan. Elvis doesn't speak much English but he has incredible
energy, good vibes, and he drums like crazy. This video tells him: we see you,
we love your vibe, and here are ways we can connect.

The video is in English but uses simple words, repetition, rhythm, and visual
hooks so a French speaker can follow along. Think: more music than lecture.
More feeling than information.


FORMAT
------
NotebookLM-style: two AI hosts riffing on a "document" — but the document is
Elvis himself. The hosts pass the mic to spike.land personas who each throw
Elvis a hook — a short, direct, connecting phrase. The rhythm should feel like
a drum circle: each persona drops in, says their piece, and the beat continues.

Duration: 4-6 minutes
Tone: Warm, playful, zero pressure, celebratory
Language: Simple English. Short sentences. Present tense. No jargon.
Music: Light percussion underneath. Build to a drum moment at the end.

HOOKS FOR CONNECTION (practical)
================================================================================

These are real ways Elvis and the people around him can connect,
no complex English needed:

 1. RHYTHM GAMES — tap a rhythm, he taps it back. No words needed.
 2. FRENCH MUSIC — play Daft Punk, Stromae, MC Solaar. Shared ground.
 3. CALL AND RESPONSE — someone hums, Elvis drums along. Universal.
 4. DRAWING + DRUMMING — someone draws while he provides the beat.
 5. COOKING RHYTHM — kitchen activities have rhythm. Do it together.
 6. COUNTING IN FRENCH — learn beats in French: un, deux, trois, quatre.
 7. BODY PERCUSSION — stomp, clap, snap. Everyone can join. No instruments.
 8. PHONE TRANSLATION — Google Translate voice mode for conversations.
 9. TEACH FRENCH — let Elvis be the teacher. Reverses the power dynamic.
10. DRUM CIRCLE — common room. Start one. Inclusion needs no translator.
