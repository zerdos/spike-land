# Founder Succession & Account Audit Plan

## 1. Critical Account Audit Checklist
All critical infrastructure and vendor accounts must be owned by a `hello@spike.land` or `admin@spike.land` alias.

- [ ] **Cloudflare:** Moved to company email.
- [ ] **GitHub Organization:** `spike-land` org created, trusted advisor added as backup admin.
- [ ] **Stripe:** Account ownership verified under company email.
- [ ] **Anthropic / OpenAI API:** API keys generated from a company-wide account.
- [ ] **Domain Registrar:** `spike.land` domain ownership registered to the company entity.
- [ ] **Password Manager:** Vault created containing all root credentials.

## 2. Emergency Succession Plan (For Investors)
"We have structured the company to mitigate single-founder risk from day one. 
1. **Architecture:** Serverless infrastructure requiring near-zero daily maintenance. 
2. **Documentation:** Up-to-date critical runbook covering deployments and recovery.
3. **Handover:** I have two identified senior engineers in my immediate network who have reviewed the codebase and can confidently take over maintenance within one week."
