# Style Profile: Reboot Protocol
# Inspiration: Daft Punk, Steve Jobs (Minimalism/Precision), Weval

1. **Sonic Texture (Daft Punk Influence):**
   - High-fidelity vocoder processing on vocals.
   - 126 BPM funk-infused disco basslines.
   - Crunchy analog side-chain compression.

2. **Aesthetic Philosophy (Steve Jobs Influence):**
   - Structural minimalism: remove all unnecessary elements.
   - Precision-engineered frequency balance.
   - "It just works" rhythmic flow: zero latency in impact.

3. **Atmospheric Depth (Weval Influence):**
   - Melancholic, cinematic synth layering.
   - Organic, slightly detuned textures contrasting with the digital crispness.
   - Emotive, evolving chord progressions that build through the track.

4. **Production Notes (69 Rounds of Iteration):**
   - Round 1-23: Establish the robotic foundation.
   - Round 24-46: Apply the "Apple-style" reductionist polish.
   - Round 47-69: Infuse the Weval-style emotive, atmospheric drift.
